CampusKart AI

AI-powered full-stack e-commerce platform with personalized product search, intelligent caching, and real-time commerce capabilities. Built with React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, and Gemini API integration.

Live Demo

Project Overview

CampusKart AI is an advanced AI-powered e-commerce platform designed for campus communities. The platform leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized product recommendations and intelligent search capabilities, enhancing the shopping experience for users.

The project features a complete authentication system, product listing with real-time inventory management, advanced cart workflows, and an innovative Interview Intelligence module that analyzes and categorizes placements experiences to help students prepare for interviews. Built with a focus on performance optimization and user experience.
